About Me

Who I Am

A passionate learner, builder and problem solver from Rangpur, Bangladesh.

I'm Md Fahim Ahammed, a Computer Science and Technology (CST) student at Rangpur Polytechnic Institute (Session 2024–2025). I have a strong interest in programming, problem solving, and building modern web applications.

I actively practice competitive programming in C and solve problems on Codeforces to sharpen my understanding of algorithms and data structures. I currently pursue the CSE Fundamentals with Phitron course (Batch 9) to further strengthen my core CS foundation.

I have completed a Web Design & Development course at Hashtag Research & Technology Ltd, and earned a certificate from the National Skills Development Authority (NSDA).

Outside of code, I love playing football ⚽, exploring new places, and simply enjoying life in Rangpur.
